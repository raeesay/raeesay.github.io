About

Hi! I am Raeesa and am a doctoral researcher at Helmholtz Munich, and am currently exploring the use of agentic AI in clinical trials and the broader landscape of health information systems. My work focuses on measuring and taxonomizing information distortion in LLM-generated text, to understand and mitigate the risks of misinformation in clinical and health-related contexts.

Before joining Helmholtz Munich, I completed my Master’s in Data and Computer Science at Heidelberg University, where I did my master's thesis on leveraging LLMs for plain language translation of medical information, focussing on readability.
